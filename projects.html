Project Usecase

Industrial Orchard Packaging
The project focused on the development and deployment of a preception line and manipulation stack to detect and place apples. The apples were to be placed in several different types of packaging with the most vibrant colors facing up. This prototype involved using an Ufactory xARM5 for manipulation and a ZED2 Depth-Camera for the perception stack.
